Timeline of Bill Cipher

  1. Bill’s Origin:

    • Bill Cipher originates from a two-dimensional dimension called Euclydia
    • He destroys his home dimension and becomes an interdimensional demon.

  2. Summoning by Fort Pines:

    • Over thirty years before the events of Gravity Falls, Ford Pines summons Bill Cipher while researching the supernatural.
    • Bill tricks Ford into building a portal that would allow him to enter the real world.

  3. First Appearance in Gravity Falls:

    • Bill makes his first physical appearance in the episode “Dreamscaperers” where he enters the minds of the Pines family.

  4. Weirdmageddon:

    • Bill initiates Weirdmageddon, a chaotic event where he brings his dimension’s chaos into Gravity Falls.
    • He turns the town into a nightmare realm and captures Ford Pines.

  5. Final Confrontation:

    • In the series finale “Weirdmageddon 3: Take Back The Falls”, the Pines family and their friends confront Bill.
    • Bill attempts to gain access to Ford’s mind to learn the secrets of the universe.

  6. Petrification:

    • Bill is ultimately defeated when Stan Pines allows Bill to enter his mind, and Ford uses the Memory Gun to erase Stan’s memories, effectively erasing Bill from existence.
    • Bill’s petrified form is seen in the aftermath, symbolizing his defeat.
